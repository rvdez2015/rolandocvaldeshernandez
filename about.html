About Me

Professional Profile

I combine long-standing classroom practice with technical expertise in programming, data, AI-supported planning, interactive dashboards and curriculum design. My work includes teaching from foundational digital literacy through to GCSE and A Level Computer Science.

Professional Strengths

  • KS2–KS5 Computer Science teaching
  • GCSE and A Level curriculum delivery
  • Python, Java, Scratch, Sphero, databases and web technologies
  • Assessment, reporting and intervention
  • Boarding, pastoral care and safeguarding awareness

Professional Identity

Teacher, tutor, mentor, curriculum developer and technology practitioner with a strong commitment to structured learning, high expectations and inclusive classroom practice.

Teaching Approach

My teaching is based on clear explanations, modelling, retrieval practice, adaptive support and purposeful assessment. I aim to make Computing accessible while also challenging pupils to think logically, solve problems independently and use subject vocabulary accurately.
